Why Tutoring?

Tutoring is a hobby I developed while interacting with classmates in school. The more I explained concepts to classmates and group members, the better I the understood the topic, and the more confidence I gained.

As I kept teaching friends at my early years in high school and later in university, I learned that anyone is cable of solving a given problem but needs a bit of help to do so given the limited interaction with teachers and professors.
